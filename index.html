Free Grace Theology

What is Free Grace Theology?

Romans 3:24

24 Being justified freely by his grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus:

Free Grace Theology consists of Christians who are united in holding to the purity of the Gospel, which is that faith alone in Jesus Christ as Savior grants Eternal Life. We stand for Eternal Security, and against additions to the Gospel message, such as Lordship Salvation, and Baptismal Regeneration.
